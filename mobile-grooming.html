Mobile Grooming Pet Salon

Gentle Hands brings the salon to you!

Each full service mobile grooming van is custom designed with safety equipment, air conditioning, heat, electricity, and water, and relies on no outside electrical or water hookups. Our vans' spacious interior helps pets maneuver easily and enhances the groomer's ability to ensure all of the pet's needs are met and their safety is assured. Gentle Hands has certified and AKC trained master groomers who pamper your pets with personal, one-on-one attention. We have animal behavior training and years of hands on experience, so you can be assured that your pet is in the most Gentle Hands.

We provide you with all same services as a stationary grooming salon. But what makes our mobile grooming salon different is that we are 100% cage free! Our products are eco-friendly, non-irritating and completely customized for your pets' individual skin and coat needs. Our services are not a la carte, so there are no hidden fees. We believe every pet should receive the best personalized care with out trying to up sell our customers. We do not work on a commission basis, this helps ensure the best groom at a competitive price.​​
